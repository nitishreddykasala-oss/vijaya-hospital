About Us

Committed to Quality Healthcare

Vijaya Hospital, located in the heart of Shankarpally, Telangana, has been a trusted name in healthcare for the local community. Our hospital was established with a vision to provide accessible, affordable, and advanced medical care to every patient who walks through our doors.

We take pride in our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and support staff who work tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives personalized attention and the best possible treatment. Our modern infrastructure includes advanced diagnostic labs, fully equipped operation theatres, a well-stocked pharmacy, and a comfortable inpatient facility.

At Vijaya Hospital, we believe that healthcare goes beyond just treatment. We focus on preventive care, health education, and building lasting relationships with our patients. Whether it's a routine check-up or a complex surgical procedure, we are here to serve you with compassion and excellence.

Our Departments

Comprehensive medical departments with expert specialists

General Medicine

Expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions including fever, infections, diabetes, hypertension, and chronic diseases.

Orthopaedics

Advanced orthopaedic care for fractures, joint replacements, sports injuries, spine disorders, and musculoskeletal conditions.

Gynaecology

Complete women's healthcare including prenatal care, delivery, fertility treatments, and management of gynaecological conditions.

Paediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents including vaccinations, growth monitoring, and childhood illnesses.

General Surgery

Minimally invasive and advanced surgical procedures for appendicitis, hernia, gallstones, and other surgical conditions.

ENT

Expert treatment for ear, nose, and throat disorders including hearing problems, sinus issues, and throat infections.

Dermatology

Professional skin care treatments for acne, eczema, psoriasis, allergies, and cosmetic dermatology procedures.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including ECG, echocardiography, heart disease prevention, and cardiac rehabilitation.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and recovery programs for post-surgery, sports injuries, chronic pain, and mobility disorders.

Emergency Medicine

Rapid emergency response with trained trauma care team available 24/7 for critical and life-threatening situations.
